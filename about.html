Our Story

Cultivating Excellence, Harvesting Purity.

Rooted in Passion

Novara Natural was born from a simple yet profound belief: nature provides everything we need for a healthy, vibrant life. Nestled in the lush hills where misty mornings greet verdant tea gardens, our journey began with a mission to share the authentic flavors of our land with the world.

We partner directly with local farmers who share our dedication to ethical cultivation. By eschewing harsh chemicals and embracing traditional farming wisdom combined with modern sustainable practices, we ensure that every leaf of tea and every slice of fruit retains its natural essence.
